Experiment to compare Marking and Radial menus


Dear Participant!

Thank you very much for agreeing to participate in the experiment.

The researcher requests your consent for participation in this study of evaluation of two novel interaction technologies: Marking Menu and Radial Menu.

For both kind of menu 48 trails are designed with different menu depth, menu breadth and device types. In each trail, you need to try to select the item as indicated in the "Please select column".
